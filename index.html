About Clarah's Café

Founded to bring a warm neighborhood coffee experience to Eldoret, Clarah's Café blends great coffee, homemade baked goods and a peaceful place to meet. Whether you're working, catching up with friends, or enjoying family time, our space is designed to feel like a home away from home. We take pride in top-notch service and using fresh ingredients from trusted local suppliers.
